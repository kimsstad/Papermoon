The Studio

Who We Are

Papermoon is a South African web studio creating thoughtful, design-led websites for artists, brands, and small businesses. Our work balances visual sensitivity with clean, reliable frontend build.

Papermoon is run by Kim and Dan, working closely together on every project.

The
Process

01

Discovery

We start by understanding your business, your goals, and what makes you different. This helps us design something that actually fits you.

02

Art Direction

We define how your website will look and feel, from fonts and colours to imagery, so everything feels consistent and intentional.

03

Technical Architecture

We build the website to be fast, reliable, and easy to use. It's not just about looks, it needs to work smoothly on every device.

04

Refinement

We fine-tune the details. Small touches like animations and interactions that make the experience feel polished and complete.
