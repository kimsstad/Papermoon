Privacy Policy

We only collect the information needed to reply to your enquiry and continue the conversation with you. We keep things simple, respectful, and limited to what is necessary for a small studio website.

Last updated: March 26, 2026

What we collect

If you contact us through the website, we collect the details you choose to send through the form: your name, your email address, and your message.

How we collect it

We collect this information only when you fill in and submit the contact form on our website.

Why we collect it

We use your information to respond to your enquiry, answer questions, and discuss a possible project with you. We do not collect information for unrelated marketing activity.

Sharing your information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing or commercial use.

How we protect it

We take reasonable steps to keep the information you send us secure and to limit access to it. No website or email system can promise absolute security, but we aim to handle your information carefully and responsibly.

Your choices

If you would like us to delete the personal information you have sent through our website, you can email us and request removal. We will do our best to respond promptly.

Privacy questions

If you have any questions about this page or about how your information is handled, please email kimstadtherr@gmail.com.
